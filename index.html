Senior researcher at Azure Research, Cambridge. I break CPUs👻 for a living: my work targets microarchitectural side channels, hardware/software security testing, fuzzing, memory safety, and trusted execution environments.

One of my main projects is Revizor, a black-box fuzzer for finding microarchitectural vulnerabilities in CPUs. I lead both the research behind Revizor and the development of the project.

PhD from TU Dresden (advisor: Christof Fetzer, Systems Engineering group). MSc in Distributed Systems Engineering (TU Dresden) and MSc in Telecommunications (NTUU "KPI").
